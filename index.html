About

This website and campaign is a collaborative project of an international team including (PhD) students from Rome (Italy), Brugge (Suisse) and Bamberg (Germany). The purpose of this project is to create a solution for convincing people to vaccinate based on different identified prototypes. It also included the identification of key actors in the communication network on Twitter, secondary research and qualitative analysis of existing Pro- and Anti-Vaccination campaigns.
